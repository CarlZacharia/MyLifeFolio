One place. Every detail. Exactly as much as you want.

MyLifeFolio is a comprehensive life documentation platform built around a simple truth: when something happens to you, the people you love shouldn't have to search for answers.

Whether you want to capture just the essentials or document every facet of your life — your story, your finances, your medical history, your wishes — MyLifeFolio grows with you.

Your Rules. Your Pace.

You decide how much to share and how deep to go. Start with basic contact information and a few key documents, or build a complete life record over time. MyLifeFolio is as simple or as comprehensive as you choose to make it — and it's always there when your family needs it most.
