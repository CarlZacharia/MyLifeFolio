Introduction

What Is
MyLifeFolio?

MyLifeFolio is your single source of truth — the platform where you document, organize, and protect everything that matters about your life, your wishes, and your legacy.

From vital records and financial accounts to personal wishes, healthcare directives, and end-of-life preferences — it is all in one place, with precisely controlled access for the people who need it.

"The document, the account, the wish, the story — always where it needs to be, always in the hands of the right person."

Platform-Wide Features
Platform Feature — Access Control

Controlled Family Access Share exactly what you choose. Nothing more.

One of MyLifeFolio's most powerful features is the ability to grant carefully controlled access to family members, friends, advisors, or anyone else you choose — giving each person a window into only the portions of your information you want them to see.

For each section of MyLifeFolio, you decide the access level. You can keep something entirely private — visible to you alone — or open specific sections to specific people. You are in complete control of who sees what, section by section.

When you grant someone access, you simply associate their email address with the sections you have chosen to share. When that person needs to view your information — during a health crisis, to help manage your affairs, or simply because you have asked them to — they visit MyLifeFolio, enter their email address, and the system sends a secure one-time access code to that email. When they enter the code, MyLifeFolio identifies them, looks up exactly what you have authorized them to see, and displays only that information. Nothing else. Nothing more.

There is no separate password to create or remember. There is no risk of accidentally exposing sections you meant to keep private. The system enforces your choices automatically.

  • Adult child assisting with care Share your Medical History and Healthcare Directives — without giving access to your financial accounts.
  • Estate planning attorney Share your Financial Overview and Personal Property — without exposing your Legacy Letters or personal Reflections.
  • Trusted neighbor Share your emergency contacts and pet care instructions — while keeping everything else completely private.
  • Sibling Share your Funeral and Memorial preferences — so they know your wishes when the time comes.

The result is a system that works the way you actually think about information sharing: not all-or-nothing, but carefully calibrated to each relationship and each need.

Platform Feature — Credential Security

Credential Vault Secure access to your online life — when your family needs it most.

The Credential Vault is a dedicated, encrypted space for storing the login credentials your family will need to access your online accounts — after you are gone, or if you become unable to manage them yourself. These are not just passwords. They are the keys to significant portions of your financial, personal, and creative life that exist entirely online.

For each account, store the service name, username or email address, password, two-factor authentication method, recovery phone or email, and any special access notes. Without this information, families routinely lose access to priceless photographs, significant financial accounts, and meaningful digital content — permanently.

Platform Feature — Custom Reporting

Custom Reports Share precisely what each person needs — nothing more, nothing less.

The Custom Reports feature gives you the ability to create tailored, shareable snapshots of your MyLifeFolio information — built around exactly what a specific person or situation requires.

The level of control is granular. You can build a report that includes an entire section — say, your complete Financial Overview — or go deeper and select only specific categories within a section, or even individual items within a category. Want to share your bank accounts and retirement accounts but not your investment accounts or life insurance? You can do that. Want to include only the medications list from your Medical History, without sharing your diagnoses or surgical history? That is possible too. You define the report; the platform assembles it.

Once configured, save your report with a name of your choosing. That saved report is always available to regenerate on demand — reflecting your most current information every time it is produced. Create as many report configurations as you need, each tailored to a different recipient or purpose.

  • For your estate planning attorney Financial Overview, Real Estate, Business Interests, Personal Property, and Beneficiary Designations — everything needed to review your plan, without personal medical or legacy content.
  • For your primary care physician Current medications, allergies, conditions and diagnoses, and healthcare directives — nothing more.
  • For a successor trustee or executor A comprehensive report: financial accounts, real estate, business interests, digital assets, income and obligations, and the location of key legal documents.
  • For an adult child assisting with your care Medical history, current physicians, healthcare preferences, long-term care wishes, and emergency contacts.
  • For a family meeting A high-level overview — household information, key contacts, and general financial structure — enough context without sensitive details.
  • For yourself A complete, full-platform report serving as your personal master reference document.

Security & Data Protection
Your Data Is Safe

How We Protect
Everything You Entrust to Us

You are being asked to store some of the most personal information you own — family details, financial accounts, medical records, legal documents, end-of-life wishes, and online credentials. We take that responsibility with the seriousness it deserves. Here is a transparent account of the protections we have implemented at every layer of the platform.

🌐
Infrastructure & Network Protection

All traffic is routed through an enterprise-grade global security network with DDoS protection, a Web Application Firewall (WAF) blocking OWASP Top 10 threats, bot protection on all login forms, and always-on HTTPS with automatic redirect of unencrypted requests.

🗄️
Row-Level Database Security

Every data table enforces security at the individual row level — at the database itself, not just the application layer. The database ensures you can only read, create, update, or delete your own data. Even if application code had a bug, the database would still block unauthorized access.

🔐
Three Layers of Encryption

Layer 1: Encryption in transit (HTTPS). Layer 2: Encryption at rest — all stored data is encrypted even if physical storage were compromised. Layer 3: Client-side field encryption for the Credential Vault — passwords are encrypted in your browser before they ever leave your device, using the same class of encryption used by governments and financial institutions.

🔑
Your Passphrase, Your Key

Encryption keys for the Credential Vault are derived from a passphrase only you know. We never see or store it. Without it, the encrypted data is unreadable — even to us. A one-time recovery key is generated at setup; if you lose both, we cannot decrypt your vault. This is by design.

✅
Authentication & Session Security

Email verification is required before access. Secure one-time codes eliminate password reuse risk. Re-authentication is required to access sensitive sections — protecting against someone accessing an unlocked device. Token-based sessions cannot be forged.

👁️
Family Access Audit Trail

Every family member access is logged with date, time, who accessed, and which sections they viewed. Highly sensitive data like Social Security numbers is automatically masked even for authorized family members. Access can be revoked with a single click, effective immediately.

💳
PCI Level 1 Payment Security

All billing is handled by a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor — the highest level of payment security certification in the industry. Your card numbers and billing details never touch our servers or database.

🛡️
Document & File Storage

Uploaded documents are stored in private, non-public storage — no publicly accessible URLs. Each user's files are isolated from all others. Only permitted file types can be uploaded. All stored files are encrypted at rest.

Security at a Glance

Your data is protected by a global security network with DDoS protection and a web application firewall · row-level security enforced at the database on every table · three layers of encryption: in transit, at rest, and client-side field encryption for your most sensitive credentials · re-authentication requirements for sensitive sections · comprehensive browser security policies · PCI Level 1 payment processing · a complete audit trail of all family access · secret keys that are never exposed to the browser.

A candid note: No platform in the world can promise 100% absolute security, and anyone who makes that claim is not being truthful. What we can promise is that we have implemented industry-leading practices at every layer of this platform — from the network edge to the database row. We continuously monitor for emerging threats, apply security updates promptly, and treat your data with the same care we would want for our own families' most personal information.
